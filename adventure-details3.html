Volleyball / Basketball

Young athletes are training in Europe!

Szklarska Poręba is the largest climatic station in Lower Silesia, known and appreciated since the mid-nineteenth century. The climatic conditions of the city are comparable to the values of alpine towns located at an altitude of 2000 m above sea level This was discovered by athletes. Polish cadres often visit the city at fitness camps. Frequent winds and terrain lead to a very quick exchange of air masses. The peat bogs of the Jizera Mountains create a specific microclimate.  The city is a center of winter sports, as snow cover remains here on average 110 days a year. The center of downhill skiing is the Szrenica massif.

Cross-country skiers dominate in the Izera Mountains, in the region of Jakuszyce. Interestingly, the treatment process in the centers in Szklarska Poręba was faster than in the alpine areas. And today this wonderful microclimate is very often used by Polish sportsmen who come here for fitness camps.
