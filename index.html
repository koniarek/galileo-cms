Galileo Lifelong Learning Centre offers numerous development opportunities for students, university employees, lecturers and many other groups from around the world, who dream of science beyond their country. However, GLL Centre is an intermediary organisation is an organisation active in the labour market or in the sectors of education, training and youth. It is a partner in a national mobility consortium.

It is the organization in charge of receiving foreign students, learners or staff and offering them a program of activities, or benefiting from a teaching / training activity.

Erasmus + Program aims to strengthen the personal development of new skills and to increase the opportunities regardless of the person, age and educational background. As a receiving institution, we play an important role in the realization of Erasmus + projects. We ensure that students benefit from the development of their professional experience with the best education programs. In addition, we provide our guests to enjoy various cultural excursions and entertainment programs.

It doesn't stop here! The Galileo lifelong centre supports young prospective students in the University enrolment process in the Republic of Poland. It chooses the most suitable universities for the candidates and stands by them in the difficulties they will encounter.

It is very important for us that our students have a voice in social, cultural and sports fields as well as in the academic field. In line with their interests, we organize international sports camps to train students to guide the era and future in which they live.
