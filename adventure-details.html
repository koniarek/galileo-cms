CANOEING / KAYAKING / YACHTING / ROWING !

Ogonki a village in Poland located in the Warmińsko-Mazurskie voivodship, in the Węgorzewo district, in the Węgorzewo commune, on the isthmus between the lakes Święcajty and Stręgiel. The Sapina River, which connects these lakes, flows through the village. Calm and chilli place for expanding skills and gaining more experience in your favourite sport.

There you can find your place, and fall in love in polish lakes during training kayaking or yachting! Huge area which lets you know more about nature, and visit the expansive Masurian lake district. However, People who are attending the ‘Galileo Center’ they benefit from many opportunities throughout the camp. Galileo Lifelong Center provides students with the necessary equipment for guidance and sports activities throughout the camp. Athletes just need to bring their beliefs with them and Galileo will create the necessary motivation for them! Not only that! Contact us if you want to participate in a tournament in any European country!

Length

From 7 days up to 20 and more!

What will be provided?

  • At least 2,5 hours a day of training on the special sport area with a professional team / trainer. For juniors and seniors
  • Accomodation (maximum 3 person room with private bathroom)
  • Transport from the airport.
  • We can organize extra catering ( traditional polish / vege / normal )
  • Sightseeing tour with translator on boat or walking or minicar / english - turkish / polish - turkish / polish - english
  • Fun day: boat trip / racing car simulator / motocross / paintball / zoo / museum / traditional food tour etc.
  • Contact with the coordinator 12 h a day
  • Invitation letter for visa

What should you expect?

We will do our best to fulfil your expectations.

We are sure that, after being a participant of our camp / study tour you will visit Wrocław a few times.

Or maybe you would like to start studying in Wrocław or another polish city for the whole year? Our camps are only the beginning of your friendship with Poland! Feel free to contact and ask any questions! Just visit Wrocław!
