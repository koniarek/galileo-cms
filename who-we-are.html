Who We Are

Alp Can Canöz


I have always devoted myself to voluntary youth work since I was young. The decisions I make on this path and the people who support me have always broadened my horizons.

At the core of our foundation’s work is the idea that every person deserves the chance to live a healthy and productive life. There is no question that this new decade is beginning at a time of tremendous unrest and uncertainty around the world. Pandemics, global warming, economic crises. That’s why because we are at a time when education is the only solution.

I have always felt enthusiastic about education. After graduate my bachelor degree in Turkey, I studied my master degree in international tourism and hospitality management in Poland.

I have participated in various activities as a young volunteer at Adana Youth Centre since 2008, During this period, I gained great experience in youth work. The experiences I have gained during my volunteer work are the building blocks of my current business.

However, during my undergraduate education, I have been involved in many Erasmus projects, in the management of the Erasmus community, and in project writing. During my master education, I have conducted various tourism and youth organizations in Europe, and we still continue these studies with my partner Marcin.

First of all my family and my dear teachers who always support me; I would like to thank Ms. Senem Açıkgöz, Dr.Mevlüt Aydoğmuş and all my teachers whose name I could not write.

Marcin Małkiewicz


I am a graduate of Tourism and Recreation with a specialisation in Organisation of Tourist Enterprises at the Academy of Physical Education in Wrocław. From childhood, I was reading mythologies and books about ancient religions, I was interested in many cultures and beliefs, especially the ancient ones.

At the same time, I was interested in the countries of the Near and Far East. Traveling around them was one of my first dreams that I managed to make a few years ago. On a daily basis, I run a company that deals with all types of work at height.

Moreover, I am a Snowboard Instructor (SITS) and an international Kitesurfing Instructor (IKO). I have been practicing many other sports for over a decade: skiing, squash, yoga, frisbee ultimate, cycling and swimming. I also love cooking, which is also related to the journeys that I go on to look for and learn new flavours.


It gives me great pleasure to take on new challenges and face them. As a result, each day is a new step forward.

The moments spent on the road and learning about new world views give me a lot of energy and inspiration to act and implement new ideas.

Seeing the world around me outside the box and noticing connections that many would not have thought of is one of my unconditional behaviours.

That is why, together with my partner Alp, I decided to set up our company Galileo Lifelong Learning Center.

The contacts I have made throughout my life can eventually create a larger structure. This network of mutual connections, created by ambitious, innovative entrepreneurs who want to broaden their horizons and gain more experience, inspires me to act. The idea arose as a result of the growing interest of Turkish students and teachers in our country.

As a new entity on the market, created by young, ambitious and freshly perceived entrepreneurs, our task is to offer services that are innovative and at the highest level.
