Camping

Ogonki is one of our most important camping sites in Poland, in the Wskgorzewo region, in the Wgorzewo commune, on the isthmus between lakes Święcajty and Stręgiel, in the Warmińsko- Mazurskie voyvodship. Although the region is generally suitable for summer sports, there are various attractions where you can do winter sports.

We generally recommend our Ogonki camp area to athletes engaged in water sports. In addition, there are personal training camps we have prepared for our young students. In these camps, students are intertwined with nature in an international environment, receiving diverse sports training and personal development training.


Of course it doesn't end there!

Throughout the camp, you will have the opportunity to see nature and cycling trips, canoe trips on the Masurian lake and observe rare birds for our students and athletes.

You will have the opportunity to report the summary of the day around the nature fire when it gets dark and many activities during the day!

People attending the "Galileo Center" take advantage of many opportunities throughout the camp. The Galileo Lifetime Center provides students with the necessary equipment for guiding and sporting activities throughout the camp. Athletes just need to bring their faith with them, and Galileo will create the necessary motivation for them! Not only that! If you want to participate in a tournament in any European country, don't forget to contact us!

It's not over yet


In our Ogonki camp area you will have various excursions where you can find historical scenes. For example, you will be able to visit "Wolf's Lair", where Hitler was hiding in the second world war, with a guide.

Length

Our camping days consist of at least 4 days.

What will be provided?

  • At least 2,5 hours a day of training on the special sport area with a professional team / trainer. For juniors and seniors
  • Accomodation (maximum 3 person room with private bathroom)
  • Transport from the airport.
  • We can organize extra catering ( traditional polish / vege / normal )
  • Sightseeing tour with translator on boat or walking or minicar / english - turkish / polish - turkish / polish - english
  • Fun day: boat trip / racing car simulator / motocross / paintball / zoo / museum / traditional food tour etc.
  • Contact with the coordinator 12 h a day
  • Invitation letter for visa

What is the benefit?

We will do our best to fulfil your expectations.

The trainings you will receive and the experiences you will experience during the camp will be an unforgettable memory. The memories you will spend here and the education you will receive will provide you with self-confidence in your social life. With it you will learn a foreign language! You will make new friendships that will taste another culture.

We are sure that, after being a participant of our camp / study tour you will visit Wrocław a few times. Or maybe you would like to start studying in Wrocław or another polish city for the whole year? Our camps are only the beginning of your friendship with Poland! Feel free to contact and ask any questions! Just visit Wrocław.
